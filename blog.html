What is Salama's Ecomerce

The Salama Ecomerce platform is a platform that is built to ensure that the customers and farmers are able to link with one another .It seeks to ensure that farmers are not taken advantage of , by middlemen.

The goal is to also ensure that the farmer is able to get quality information about the markets an dthe consumer preferences .We also seek to be custodians of private information between the customers.

Vision

We seek to partner with farmers with consumer, provide market updates and promote best farming practices
